Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

When you submit a review through this form, we collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • WhatsApp mobile number
  • Star rating
  • Written feedback / review text
  • Recommended staff member name
  • Date and time of submission

How We Use Your Data

Your data is stored securely in a private Google Sheet accessible only to The Coffee House management. We use it to:

  • Improve our service quality based on your feedback
  • Recognise and reward staff members
  • Contact you regarding your reward or special offers (via WhatsApp)

Google Reviews

When you choose to post on Google, your review text is copied to your clipboard and Google Reviews is opened in a new tab. We do not control what you post on Google — that is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties outside of The Coffee House team.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for up to 12 months for service improvement purposes, after which it may be anonymised or deleted.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related queries, please speak to a manager at The Coffee House or contact us via WhatsApp.
