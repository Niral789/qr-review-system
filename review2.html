Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

When you submit feedback through this form, we collect:

  • Your name & contact number
  • Star rating & written feedback
  • Recommended team member
  • Date and time of submission

How We Use Your Data

Your data is stored securely in a private system accessible only to The Hunger Station management, used to improve service quality and recognise team members.

Public Reviews

If you choose to share your review publicly, we show you your review text and you can copy and paste it yourself. Opening the review site is always your own choice.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third parties outside The Hunger Station team.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for up to 12 months for service improvement purposes.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related queries, please speak to a manager at The Hunger Station.
