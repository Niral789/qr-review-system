Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

When you submit a review through this form, we collect:

  • Your name & Mobile number
  • Star rating & written feedback
  • Recommended staff member
  • Date and time of submission

How We Use Your Data

Your data is stored securely in a private database accessible only to The Hunger Station management, used to improve service quality and recognise staff.

Public Reviews

When you choose to post publicly, your review text is copied to your clipboard and the review site is opened in a new tab.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third parties outside The Hunger Station team.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for up to 12 months for service improvement purposes.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related queries, please speak to a manager at The Hunger Station.
